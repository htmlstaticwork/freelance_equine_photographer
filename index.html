About Elena

A Lifetime Devoted to
Horses & Light

Professional equine photographer based globally. Elena started photographing horses at the age of 12, blending her passion for these majestic creatures with a deep fascination for the interplay of light and shadow.

Trained in fine art photography, her work goes beyond mere documentation. She captures the soul, power, and elegance of each horse. Trusted by Olympic riders, prestigious studs, and historic ranches worldwide.
